Pollution Overview

What is pollution?

Pollution refers to the introduction of harmful substances or products into the environment, which can cause adverse effects on human health, wildlife, and the planet's ecosystems. It occurs when pollutants—substances that contaminate air, water, or soil—are released into the environment in quantities that exceed its natural capacity to assimilate or neutralize them.
